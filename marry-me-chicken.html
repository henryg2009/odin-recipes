Marry Me Chicken

Marry me chicken is a sauteed chicken in a creamy sun-dried tomato. This is a dish best served with your favorite pasta.

This specific recipe was submitted from thedailygourment on allrecipes.com.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • ¼ teaspoon ground thyme
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ cup chicken broth, divided
  • 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup of Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup whipping cream
  • ¼ cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 pinch of red pepper flakes(if desired)
  • salt to taste

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Place the chicken breasts on a flat work surface. Slice horizontally through the middle, be careful not to cut all the way through to the other side. Open the 2 sides and spread them out like an open book to butterfly.

  3. Melt butter in a large, oven-safe skilled over medium-high heat. Add garlic, oregano, thyme. Saute until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

  4. Add chicken and cook until golden brown but not fully cooked, 3 to 4 minutes per side.

  5. Pour ¼ cup of chicken broth into the skilled and bake in the preheated oven until the chicken is no longer pink in the center, roughly 15 minutes.

  6. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the angel hair pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, according to cooking instructions.

  7. Remove skilled from the oven and transfer chicken to plate, reserving juices in the skillet. Place the skilled on the stovetop.

  8. Whisk flour into the skilled over medium heat. Add remaining chicken broth, Parmesan cheese, and whipping cream. Whisk until combined.

  9. Add sundried tomatoes, red pepper flakes, and salt. Stir well. Add chicken back into the skillet.

  10. Serve on top of cooked pasta. Add more parmesan cheese as you like.

  11. Enjoy!
